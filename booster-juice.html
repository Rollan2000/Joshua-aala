UX App: Booster Juice (Web/Mobile App)

Mobile Web

Overview

Redesigning an app’s concept and delivery method

Roles

UX/UI designer, UX researcher

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Adobe XD

Research Proposal: Redesign Booster Juice’s mobile app so users can order drinks online quickly and conveniently.

Problems: The booster juice app has a lot of errors in the previous version. Redesigning this app that serves to solve the problem that will allow users to view and purchase kinds of drinks like juices, smoothies, and slushies.

Solution: A better customer experience is achieved by providing users with an improved app that lets them see what refreshments they want before they make a purchase.

Responsibilities: Design sprint analysis, User Research & Analysis, Brand Design, UI Design & Prototyping, and Usability Testing.

Design Thinking Process: This Design Thinking process has helped me to understand better and to proceed to my XD prototype for UX design: Understand →Explore → Materialize

Process: I began to make Wireframes for mobile, User flow, and Style Tiles to demonstrate our unique design ideas, Logo Brand and colour Palette. Finally, created Low Fidelity and High Fidelity Mockups for clickable Prototype responsive in the IOS App.
