UX App: Booster Juice (Web/Mobile App)

Tool(s) used: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Adobe XD

Mobile Web

Research Proposal: Redesign Booster Juice’s mobile app so users can order drinks online quickly and conveniently.

Problems: The Booster Juice app has a lot of errors in the previous version. Redesigning this app will solve these problems and allow users to view and purchase various drinks.

Solution: Provide users with an improved app that allows them to see available drinks before making a purchase.

Responsibilities: Design sprint analysis, User Research & Analysis, Brand Design, UI Design, and Prototyping.

Design Thinking Process: Understand → Explore → Materialize

Process: Wireframes, User flow, Style Tiles, Logo Branding, Low & High Fidelity Mockups.
