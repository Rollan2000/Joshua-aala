UX App: Booster Juice (Web/Mobile App)

Mobile Web

Overview

Redesigning an app’s concept and delivery method

Roles

UX/UI designer, UX researcher

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Adobe XD

Research Proposal: Redesign Booster Juice’s mobile app so users can order drinks online quickly and conveniently.

Problems: The Booster Juice app has a lot of errors in the previous version. Redesigning this app will solve these problems and allow users to view and purchase various drinks.

Solution: Provide users with an improved app that allows them to see available drinks before making a purchase.

Responsibilities: Design sprint analysis, User Research & Analysis, Brand Design, UI Design, and Prototyping.

Design Thinking Process: Understand → Explore → Materialize

Process: Wireframes, User flow, Style Tiles, Logo Branding, Low & High Fidelity Mockups.

Problem Statements:

-What is the problem I am trying to solve?

The booster juice app has a lot of errors in the previous version. Redesigning this app that serves to solve the problem that will allow users to view and purchase kinds of drinks like juices, smoothies, and slushies.

  • To solve this problem, I want to redesign a booster juice app so users can view/purchase all kinds of drinks in an easy-to-use manner.
  • In this app, users will be able to pay for their drinks online and pick up their drinks at the nearest branch.

    • - Design Questions:

  • What can I do to redesign an app so it’s easy for customers to purchase any drink?
  • Would it be possible to redesign the app so that it is easier to navigate to order drinks and locate them?
