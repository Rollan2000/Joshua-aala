UX/UI App: Insomnia Cookies

Tool(s) used: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma

Mobile

Research Proposal: The main purpose of this project to redesign the “Insomnia Cookies” app in order to improve usability and design overall.

Problems: The cookie cards and product features will switch to browsing mode when you tap them. It only delivers cookies in the States.

Solution: Cookies can be shipped worldwide by pressing the shipping mode button for a better customer experience. Designed new cookie selection cards.

Responsibilities: Design sprint analysis, User Research & Analysis, Brand Design, User flow, Wireframe Sketches,Logo brand redesign, UI Style Guide or Style Tile, Mid/High-Fidelity Prototype in FIGMA software

Design Thinking Process: This Design Thinking process has helped me to understand better and to proceed to FIGMA prototype for UX design: Understand →Explore → Materialize
